Career Options

Well Known

Software Engineer

Software development is a rapidly growing field that is perfect for those who enjoy problem-solving and creativity. As a software developer, you will use your coding skills to design, develop, and test software applications for a wide range of industries. Software developers work in a variety of settings, including technology companies, startups, and government agencies.

Less Known

Actuarial Scientist

Actuarial science is an exciting and rewarding career path that is perfect for those who enjoy working with numbers and analyzing data. As an actuary, you will use your mathematical and statistical skills to assess and manage financial risk. Actuaries work in a variety of industries, including insurance, finance, and are highly valued for their ability to make data-driven decisions.

Well Known

Doctor

Doctors are medical professionals who diagnose and treat illnesses and injuries. They work in a variety of settings, including hospitals, clinics, private practices, and other healthcare facilities. As a doctor, you will work with patients of all ages, providing them with medical care and advice to help them manage their health.

Less Known

Media Analyst

Media analysts follow the media coverage of a specific product or service for a company. They are responsible for conducting research to produce media evaluation reports based on how often a product or service was mentioned in the media industry. Media analysts can work at print media, radio stations, and marketing agencies.
